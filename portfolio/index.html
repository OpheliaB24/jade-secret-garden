Cafe Botannix

Botannix cafe located in Takapuna, Auckland, requested 3 large floral installations and 4 potted arrangements, with a tropical, pastel colour pallet. I created floral clouds with pale pink and greens to complement their feature wall paper. The 4 little potted arrangements displayed on their shelves are filled with more pale green and yellow blooms to create more of tropical rustic vibe.

Rustic Bohemian Wedding florals

Set at the beautiful Linden Estate Winery this wedding had a rustic yet bohemian vibe. I created a large bridal bouquet with soft pink and beige tones and touches of green to complement her stunning princes gown. To create a cohesive look, I put together four small bridesmaid bunches and four matching buttonholes for the groom and groomsmen. To tie everything together the bride asked me to create two arrangements that could be attached onto the archway. I incorporated the same colours and flora into these two pieces and added large palm leaves and Toi Toi to add create more relaxed bohemian feel.

Can I choose what flowers go into my arrangement?
Yes and no, I do my best to include any specific floral requests that you may have but keep in mind that not all flowers can be dried successfully and I only use seasonal blooms.
Can you set up and pack down my wedding flowers?
Yes, I can come and set up any floral installations and pack them down if needed. Please be aware that this comes at an extra cost depending on your location and size of your arrangements. Keep in mind that certain installations will require professional set up such as floral wall, hanging installations or arch floral arrangements etc...
Do you just supply to Whanganui?
Jade's Secret Garden is available for delivery throughout New Zealand and event and wedding installation in the Manawatu and Wellington region.
Do you do styles shoots?
Yes, I do styles shoots, weddings, custom order and lots more.
What is the deposit and how much to secure the date with you?
I charge a $200 deposit to secure the date.
Do you do workshops?
Yes, please keep a eye out on my instagram and facebook pages for upcoming events or contact me if you wish to organise a private workshop of anykind.
