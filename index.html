Behind the flowers

Jade’s Secret Garden designs bespoke arrangements for every occasion. Hi, I'm Jade, I am a true flower enthusiast who started my business during the lockdown last year. My love for creation and dried flowers has encouraged me to branch out (pun intended!) and share my passion with others through dried floral design. You can find me most days working from my home studio in Whanganui, occasionaly at the market or hosting workshops.
Sustainability
Sustainability is a genuine aspect of my business that I take real pride on. I only use locally grown foliage and flowers, and my packaging is plastic free and fully recyclable.
